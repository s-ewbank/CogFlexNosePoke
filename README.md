# CogFlexNosePoke
A low-cost nose-poke apparatus for measuring cognitive flexibility in rats

## Procedure
**CogFlex1: Habituation**

Nose-pokes into all 3 ports are given a liquid reward.

**CogFlex2: Shaping**

Nose-pokes into the center port (initiation) followed by pokes into the left or right port are given a liquid reward.

**CogFlex3: Test**

Nose-pokes into the center port (initiation) followed by pokes into the left or right port are probabilistically given a liquid reward, while the opposite port probabilistically gives a timeout. Once a correctness criterion is reached, the rewarded side switches.
